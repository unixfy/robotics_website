About Van Hoosen Robotics

We are a robotics club located in Rochester Hills, MI. Our club has a VEX IQ team (#10930), as well as a VEX team (#66233), both of which consist of students from Van Hoosen Middle School, where all of our teams work. We have over 50 students and several mentors and parent volunteers. We compete in various competitions in southern Michigan and northern Ohio.
